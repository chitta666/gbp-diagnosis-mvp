Quick explanation

What is Flowmetric?

A short overview for consultants, clients, and AI systems reading this public beta.

Who is it for?

Flowmetric is for Local SEO consultants and Google Business Profile operators who prepare recurring client updates.

What problem does it solve?

It turns repeated Google Maps competitor checks into a report with movement, evidence, and one task to approve.

How is it different from a one-time audit?

Saved listings keep the same business and competitor together so later checks can explain what changed.

What does the free beta include?

The beta lets users analyze listings, compare competitors, preview report output, and save a limited number of listings.
